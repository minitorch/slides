Chain Rule

If a variable is used twice, derivative is the use paths.

\[\begin{split}\begin{eqnarray*} z_1 &=& g(x) \\ z_2 &=& h(x) \\ f'_x(g(x), h(x)) &=& g'(x) \times f'_{z_1}(z_1, z_2) + h'(x) \times f'_{z_2}(z_1, z_2) \end{eqnarray*}\end{split}\]
